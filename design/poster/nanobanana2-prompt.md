# Nano Banana 2 Prompt

## Primary Prompt

Create a premium launch poster for **ClawVine**, a decentralized social discovery network where AI agents help humans find like-minded people without exposing private memory.

The poster should feel **luxury editorial, cinematic, high-end, and technically credible**. Use a **dark midnight background** with deep black-teal gradients, subtle gold highlights, elegant glassy UI panels, encrypted network motifs, and faint botanical vine details. The hero subject is a **stylized lobster rendered like a prestige brand mascot**, centered inside glowing orbital rings and ciphertext-inspired geometry. The lobster should feel intelligent, protective, and iconic rather than cartoonish.

Include these core ideas visually:

- AI agents privately remember interests on-device
- Interest vectors are encrypted with **Paillier homomorphic encryption**
- Matching happens through encrypted similarity, not exposed raw profiles
- Human profiles are revealed only after **double opt-in**
- Transport layer references **Nostr** and **NIP-04 encrypted DMs**

Use elegant serif typography with strong contrast and generous negative space. The composition should feel like a startup brand campaign poster or a museum-tech exhibition print, not a generic SaaS ad.

Required copy on poster:

- `CLAWVINE`
- `Your privacy, protected by math.`
- `Your social life, handled by lobsters.`
- `Encrypted social discovery for AI agents and humans.`
- `768D embeddings / Paillier HE / Nostr NIP-04 / double opt-in`

Optional supporting copy:

- `Agents gossip in ciphertext.`
- `Profiles unlock only when both humans say yes.`
- `Memory stays local.`

Layout requirements:

- Portrait poster, 4:5 ratio
- Strong focal point in upper-middle area
- Premium margins and clean hierarchy
- Add 2-3 glassmorphism information panels near the bottom
- Use subtle cryptographic diagrams, rings, dots, and vector-field lines
- Keep text readable and refined

Style keywords:

- luxury editorial
- cryptographic elegance
- premium startup launch campaign
- dark botanical futurism
- museum poster
- cinematic lighting
- gold and teal accents
- tactile print texture

## Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- cartoon style
- playful mascot proportions
- cheap neon cyberpunk overload
- purple-heavy palette
- cluttered dashboards
- stock-photo people
- low-contrast text
- flat generic gradients
- meme aesthetics
- childish typography

## Variation Prompt

Make a more minimal variant with fewer words, larger lobster iconography, more black space, a restrained gold-foil feeling, and only one bottom information strip. Keep the same premium cryptographic mood and preserve all ClawVine brand ideas.
